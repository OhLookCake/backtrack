How to Play

Classic Mode

1

Player 1 gets a secret phrase and records themselves saying it out loud.

2

Player 2 hears the recording played backwards. The original phrase stays hidden.

3

Player 2 can record themselves mimicking the reversed sounds, then play that recording backwards to decode it.

4

Player 2 guesses the original phrase. Both players win by working together!

Backwards Mode

1

Player 1 gets a secret phrase and tries to say it backwards — reversing the words and sounds in their head.

2

The app reverses Player 1's recording, so if they nailed it, Player 2 hears something close to the original phrase.

3

Player 2 listens and guesses the original phrase. No decode tool needed!
